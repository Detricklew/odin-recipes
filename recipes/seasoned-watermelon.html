Seasoned Watermelon

Description

This refreshing treat is perfect for those looking for an escape from the hot summer heat!

This is a fairly straightfoward recipe requiring very little time or prep work!

Ingredients

  • Watermelon
  • Lime (optional)
  • Tajin Seasoning

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl and knife.
  2. If watermelon is not cut already begin by cutting the watermelo into small chunks.
  3. Place chunks into bowl.
  4. If you want extra lime flavor (I love the extra lime flavor) squeeze the lime over the watermelon.
  5. Sprinkle your tajin seasoning generously over your watermelon.
  6. Enjoy your delicious summer snack!
