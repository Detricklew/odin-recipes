Ingredients

Here is what you need to craft true flavor.

  • 2 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of distill white vinegar
  • 1 bottle of red food coloring
  • For frosting
    • Cream cheese (1 box)(thawed)
    • 2 sticks of butter (room temperature)
    • 4 cups of confectioner sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Sift all the dry ingredients together in a large bowl, mix all the wet ingredients into another bowl
  3. Mix dry and wet together and until batter is smooth
  4. Divide evenly into greased and flour cake pans
  5. Bake cakes for 30 minutes, place to toothpick into the center of the cake.Â  if it comes out clean the cake is ready
  6. cake must cool down before applying the cream cheese frosting
  7. For cream cheese frosting mix all indegrients until right consistency
